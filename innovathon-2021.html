March 6th - 7th, 2021

The InNovathon is our spin on the traditional makeathon — where high school students like you will get the opportunity to pitch innovative improvements to your favorite products and services. If the past few months have taught us anything, it’s to be adaptable, flexible, and resilient. Through keynotes, networking sessions, and hands-on workshops, we aim to provide you the platform and resources you need to apply your unique skill set to new solutions.
