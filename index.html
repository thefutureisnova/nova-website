About Us

There is a long-standing notion that innovation and creative ideation are exclusive to certain business types, people, and industries. Things that come to mind? Startups, tech companies, executives. Innovating outside of these traditional constraints requires creating space for members of underrepresented groups to go against the grain. This need for change has gone unnoticed and unaddressed.

It's time for a ReinNovation. Enter Nova.

Nova envisions a professional world where young womxn are empowered to disrupt, create, and innovate in any space — from high-growth startups to legacy industries — by nurturing a community that celebrates differences while encouraging collaboration, starting meaningful and difficult conversations by sharing impactful stories, and offering hands-on experiences and access to industry professionals, resources, and tools.

We're thrilled to announce the InNovathon — our flagship event — as our foray into making our vision a reality. Unlike traditional makeathons, the InNovathon celebrates incremental and disruptive innovation by tasking participants to challenge and ideate on staples of their daily lives. Watch this space: the InNovathon is coming to high school womxn in early 2021.

In a universe where there is space for everyone to shine, why can't we all be supernovas?
