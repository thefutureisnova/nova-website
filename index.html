About Us

There is a long-standing notion that innovation and creative ideation are exclusive to certain business types, people, and industries. Things that come to mind? Startups, tech companies, executives. Innovating outside of these traditional constraints requires creating space for members of underrepresented groups to go against the grain. This need for change has gone unnoticed and unaddressed.

It's time for a ReinNovation. Enter Nova.

Nova envisions a professional world where young women are empowered to disrupt, create, and innovate in any space — from high-growth startups to legacy industries — by nurturing a community that celebrates differences while encouraging collaboration, starting meaningful and difficult conversations by sharing impactful stories, and offering hands-on experiences and access to industry professionals, resources, and tools. In past year, we've been so proud to plan our flagship makeathon, Innovathon 2021, create a community through content on Instagram and LinkedIn, host a virtual "Charting Your Path to College" series, and onboard a dynamic class of high school Ambassadors. Watch this space: The Future is Nova is bringing lots of new and exciting opportunities to you soon. We'll leave you with this — in a universe where there is space for everyone to shine, why can't we all be supernovas?
